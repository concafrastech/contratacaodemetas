TOMCAT NOVO
Instalar JDK 1.7.0_80
Baixar e descompactar Tomcat 7.0.75
Copiar ost-home, SignDocSDK, SignWare
Copiar it.olivetti.ost.server.web.test.war

* WIN32
Fazer backup da pasta TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win32/c-api/Win32/
Remover conteúdo da pasta TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win32/c-api/Win32/
Copiar conteúdo de SignWare_api.zip!/c-api/Win32/ para TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win32/c-api/Win32/
= Fazer backup do arquivo TOMCAT_HOME/lib/SPSignware-32.jar
= Remover arquivo TOMCAT_HOME/lib/SPSignware-32.jar 
Copiar arquivo de SignWare_api.zip!/jar/Win32/SPSignware_32.jar para TOMCAT_HOME/lib/ 

* WIN64
Fazer backup da pasta TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win64/c-api/Win64/
Remover conteúdo da pasta TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win64/c-api/Win64/
Copiar conteúdo de SignWare_api.zip!/c-api/Win64/ para TOMCAT_HOME/SignWare/Win64/c-api/Win64/
Fazer backup do arquivo TOMCAT_HOME/lib/SPSignware-32.jar 
= Remover arquivo TOMCAT_HOME/lib/SPSignware-32.jar 
Copiar arquivo de SignWare_api.zip!/jar/Win64/SPSignware_32.jar para TOMCAT_HOME/lib/

* APPS
= Remover a pasta TOMCAT_HOME/webapps/ostserver
= Remover a pasta TOMCAT_HOME/webapps/SignVerify
Substituir o arquivo ostserver.jar em TOMCAT_HOME/webapps/
Substituir o arquivo SignVerify.jar em TOMCAT_HOME/webapps/
